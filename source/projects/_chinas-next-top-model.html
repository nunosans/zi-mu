China’s Next Top Model

Branding and promotional material / Work for IPCN Ltd.
November 2014

China’s Next Top Model is the official Chinese adaption of the hit reality TV shows American’s Next Top Model. Some of the fashion world’s most well known experts will judge these participants. These judges, who will include high profile fashion industry gurus, will narrow the field week to week until one lucky winner will be chosen.

Because this reality show is between girls and boys. I chose the form of poker to be concept. The King and Queen represent the contestants in the show.
