Airta

Branding and promotional material / Work for Wondullful.Co.
2014

Arita was founded in 1616. Stellar Works, the furniture brand, will collaborate with Arita for celebrating its 400th anniversary. The company has invited designers Neri & Hu, Michael Anstassiades, Ilse Crawford and Ross Lovegrove to participate in this project. The aim will be to push this ancient craft and material into new product possibilities.

I designed a symbol with the form of Kamon that is Japanese emblems used to decorate or identify an individual or a family.
