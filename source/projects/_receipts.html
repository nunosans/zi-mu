Receipts

Student Project
2013

At beginning of the time in London, I collected all the receipts to manage my expenses of shopping. I found many different patterns, typography and fonts on receipts, which were attracting me. I designed a Zine about my receipts-my life in London.

In the Zine, there is a poster only with scanned time point from receipts, which is my shopping diary. The booklet is binding by receipts I chosen from my collection, having beautiful pattern. I created a recipe using the typography and fonts from receipt.
